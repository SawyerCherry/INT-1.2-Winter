About Us

In Fall of 2018 our family decided to take up donations from our community to cook and deliver a hot Thanksgiving meal to deprived families in Monroe County Kentucky

In fall of 2020 we made the ultimate decision to continue our services during the COVID-19 Pandemic. As per Governor Beshear's request we abided by CDC recommendations and guidelines. On thanksgiving day we delivered over 850 plates. On behalf of our family, we would like to thank our sponsors and those who donated groceries, money, or both! We would also like to thank those who have volunteered their time to serve their community by delivering, plating, or cooking the food!
