For our family's thanksgiving we always get together and share an excellent meal with each other. In fall of 2018 we decided that we should give back to our community. With a little help from the community, business owners, employees, anonymous donations, and hard work, we served over 700 people our first year!

We are so grateful for the support that our commuinity has shown in the past three years. We look forward to your contributions next Thanksgiving!

On behalf of the Bartley family, we would like to thank all of those who volunteered their time, or donated to Turkey The Town.

Who We are

We are a big family who have always had something to eat on thanksgiving, we would like to share that with everyone in our community!

What we do

We accept donations all year, before we deliver food on thanksgiving day, we prepare the food the day and night before. If you would like to donate to us, head over to our donate page. If you would like to volunteer to cook, deliver, or plate food, give us a call!

Our Goal

Our goal is to raise as much money as possible to deliver everyone in the Tompkinsville, Gamaliel, and Fountain Run area a meal on thanksgiving day.
